Fresh Market Delights

This site plan outlines the specifications for the Fresh Market Delights grocery website.

Site Name:

Fresh Market Delights

Reason: This name reflects the freshness and variety of products offered in a market setting, appealing to shoppers looking for quality and diversity in grocery items.

Site Purpose:

Fresh Market Delights aims to provide a convenient online platform for customers to browse and purchase fresh produce, groceries, and specialty items. The website will feature product listings, recipes, promotional offers, and a seamless shopping experience.

Scenarios:

Scenario 1: What are today's fresh produce specials?

Scenario 2: How can I place an order for home delivery?

Color Schema:

Primary Color: #36a832 (Used for headings and accents)

Secondary Color: #f2f2f2 (Used for background and contrast)

Typography:

Heading Font: Montserrat (Bold) - Used for main headings.

Body Font: Open Sans - Used for paragraphs and general text.
